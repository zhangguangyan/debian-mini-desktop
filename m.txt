3. Key Differences Summarized
Aspect	Traditional Log-Based Monitoring	OpenTelemetry & Prometheus (Metrics-Based)
Data Type	Unstructured or semi-structured logs	Structured time series metrics
Data Collection	Event-driven (individual log entries)	Periodic scraping or pushing of metric data
Data Storage	Log files or log management systems	Time series databases optimized for metrics
Analysis Focus	Detailed event context and individual occurrences	Trends, aggregations, and real-time monitoring
Performance Overhead	Higher due to log parsing and processing	Lower, as metrics are pre-structured and optimized for ingestion
Use Cases	Troubleshooting, detailed auditing, forensic analysis	Performance monitoring, alerting, capacity planning
